EX\_10FEB - Plant growth simulator

Track the growth of a plant over a given number of days.

&nbsp;- Increment the plant's height by the daily growth rate (converting millimeters to centimeters).

&nbsp;- Decrease the water supply by 50 milliliters per day (the plant's water consumption).

&nbsp;- If the water supply reaches 0, stop the plant's growth for the remaining days.

Parameters:

&nbsp;- initialHeight (int): The initial height of the plant in centimeters.

 - days (int): The number of days to simulate the growth.

 - dailyGrowth (int): The daily growth of the plant in millimeters.

 - waterSupply (int): The initial water supply in milliliters.
